WHY?

Running a website isn't free, especially one that deals with large numbers of users. Most social networks cover this cost by trading your personal information with advertisers in the form of targetted advertising, often without your consent. Murmur is different. We do not show ads anywhere on our site as we believe in our users privacy, but this means we need an alternative source of income to keep the site running.

We realise that not everyone will have the means of purchasing a premium account. However, we believe that the free standard account still retains the essence of what makes murmur special.
